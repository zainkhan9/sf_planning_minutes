February 15, 2007 Special Meeting

SAN FRANCISCO

PLANNING COMMISSION

Minutes of Special Meeting

Commission Chambers - Room 82

City Hall, 1 Dr. Carlton B. Goodlett Place

Thursday, February 15, 2007

12:00 PM

Special Meeting

COMMISSIONERS PRESENT: Alexander, Antonini, S. Lee, W. Lee, Moore, Olague, and Sugaya

THE MEETING WAS CALLED TO ORDER AT 12:30 P.M.

STAFF IN ATTENDANCE: Linda Avery â Commission Secretary.

SPEAKERS: NONE

A. EXECUTIVE SESSION

PLANNING DIRECTOR SEARCH - The Planning Commission will meet in closed session pursuant to San Francisco Administrative Code Section 67.10(b) and Government Code Section 54957(b)(1) to review resumes of candidates for the position of Director of Planning.

At the conclusion of the closed session, and pursuant to Section 67.10(b) of the San Francisco Administrative Code, the Commission will reconvene in open session and shall by motion and vote elect to:

  1. Disclose no information, or

  1. Disclose information that a majority deems to be in the public interest.

ACTION: In public session (following the closed Executive Session) the Commission, by unanimous vote, passed a motion to not disclose any information

AAYES: Alexander, Antonini, Moore, S. Lee, W. Lee, Olague, and Sugaya

Adjournment: 1:30 P.M.

THESE MINUTES WERE PROPOSED FOR ADOPTION AT THE SPECIAL MEETING OF THE PLANNING COMMISSION ON THURSDAY, May 31, 2007.

SPEAKERS: None

ACTION: Approved

AYES: Antonini, S. Lee, W. Lee, Moore, Olague, and Sugaya

ABSENT: Alexander
