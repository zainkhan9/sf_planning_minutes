SAN FRANCISCO

PLANNING COMMISSION

Meeting Minutes

Commission Chambers - Room 400

City Hall, 1 Dr. Carlton B. Goodlett Place

Thursday, January 10, 2013

     12:00 PM

Special Meeting

 

A.        CLOSED SESSION: 12:05 PM

 

1.         Public Comments on Matters to be discussed in Closed Session.

 

None

 

2.        Consideration of Adoption:  a motion on Whether to Assert the Attorney-Client Privilege Regarding the Matters Listed Below as Conference With Legal Counsel. (San Francisco Administrative Code Section 67.10(d).) 

 

ACTION:     Approved Motion to Assert the Attorney-Client Privilege

AYES:        Fong, Wu, Antonini, Borden, Hillis, Moore, Sugaya

 

THE PLANNING COMMISSION WILL GO INTO CLOSED SESSION TO DISCUSS THE FOLLOWING:

 

3.         Conference with Legal Counsel - Pursuant to California Government Code Section 54956.9(c) and San Francisco Administrative Code Section 67.10(d) to discuss enforcement and whether to initiate litigation with respect to the Academy of Art University.  Anticipated Litigation:  As Plaintiff.  (Office of the City Attorney)

 

FOLLOWING THE CLOSED SESSION, THE PLANNING COMMISSION WILL RECONVENE IN OPEN SESSION.

 

4.         Following the Closed Session, the Planning Commission will report on any action taken during the Closed Session and will consider a motion regarding whether to disclose any part of the discussions during Closed Session.

 

ACTION:     The Commission Approved a motion to not disclose the discussions during the Closed Session.

AYES:        Fong, Wu, Antonini, Borden, Hillis, Moore, Sugaya

 

Adjournment: 1:07 PM

 

 

Adopted: January 24, 2013
