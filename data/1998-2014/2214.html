HUNTERS POINT SHIPYARD PHASE 2 / CANDLESTICK POINT PROJECT - Informational update on the integrated development of Candlestick Point and Parcels B-G of the Hunters Point Shipyard. An update briefing for the Commission on the phased mixed-use development proposal for about 700 acres along San Francisco's southeast waterfront, including approximately 10,500 housing units, over 300 acres of new and restored open space and recreation areas, about 2.5 million square-feet of R&D office space, regional and neighborhood retail, rebuilding of the Alice Griffith Public Housing Development, a 220-room hotel, new and renovated Shipyard artist space, a 10,000 seat performance venue on Candlestick Point, a 300-slip marina, various supportive infrastructure and space for a 69,000 seat professional football stadium.

Preliminary Recommendation: Informational item only, no action required.
