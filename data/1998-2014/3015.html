SAN FRANCISCO

PLANNING COMMISSION

Meeting Minutes

Commission Chambers - Room 400

City Hall, 1 Dr. Carlton B. Goodlett Place

Thursday, December 8, 2011

10:00 AM

Special Meeting

 

COMMISSIONERS PRESENT: Olague, Miguel, Antonini, Borden, Fong, Moore, and Sugaya

 

THE MEETING WAS CALLED TO ORDER BY VICE PRESIDENT MIGUEL AT 10:25 A.M

 

STAFF IN ATTENDANCE: John Rahaim – Director of Planning, Scott Sanchez – Zoning Administrator, Kevin Guy, Linda Avery – Commission Secretary

 

F.            SPECIAL CALENDAR

 

1.         2007.0030M                                                                            (K. GUY: (415) 558-6163)

            8 Washington STREET - north side of Washington Street between The Embarcadero and Drumm Street; Assessor’s Block 168/Lot 58, Block 171/69, Block 201/Lot 12 and Seawall Lot 351, which includes Lot 13 - Consideration of a Resolution of Intent to Initiate an Amendment to "Map 2 - Height and Bulk Plan" within the Northeastern Waterfront Area Plan of the General Plan, to reclassify two portions of the southwestern portion of the development site from the existing 84-foot height limit to a height of 92 feet in one portion, and 136 feet in another portion. The requested action is associated with a development that proposes to demolish the existing surface parking lot and Golden Gateway Tennis and Swim Club, and construct a new health club, residential buildings ranging from four to twelve stories in height containing 145 dwelling units, ground-floor retail uses totaling approximately 20,000 square feet, and 400 off-street parking spaces.

Preliminary Recommendation:  Approve initiation

 

SPEAKERS:     Ernestine Weiss, Julia Sullivan, Stewart Morton, Nana Roth, June Osterberg, Paul Wermer, Bill Sauro, Lee Radner, Mason Grigsby, Norman Patrick Doyle, William Hannan, Nanette Leuschel, Pete Jones, Fred Allaryie, Paul Renne, Toby Levine, James Daudge, Thomas Ryan, Brad Paul, Jon Golinger, Sue Hestor

ACTION:           Approved Initiation

AYES:              Olague, Miguel, Antonini, Borden, Fong, and Sugaya

NAYES:            Moore

MOTION:           18501

 

Adjournment: 11:47 AM
