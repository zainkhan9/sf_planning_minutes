18. 95.469E (NISHIMURA)

THE SAN FRANCISCO ZOOLOGICAL GARDENS (S.F. ZOO) MASTER PLAN ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT REPORT Certification of Environmental Impact Report (EIR). 1 Zoo Road, Lots 6 and 7 in Assessor's Block 7281.
