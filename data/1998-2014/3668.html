SAN FRANCISCO

PLANNING COMMISSION

 

DRAFT Minutes of Special Hearing

 

Hearing Room 400

City Hall, 1 Dr. Carlton B. Goodlett Place

                             Thursday, October 17, 2013

 

11:30 A.M.

 

Special Meeting

COMMISSIONERS PRESENT:  President Fong, Vice President Wu, Michael Antonini, Gwyneth Borden, Rich Hillis, Kathrin Moore, Hisashi Sugaya

COMMISSIONERS ABSENT:     None

ACTING COMMISSION SECRETARY:   Linda Avery Herbert

DEPUTY CITY ATTORNEY:       Marlena Byrne

THE MEETING WAS CALLED TO ORDER BY PRESIDENT FONG AT 11:33 AM

 

A.        SPECIAL CALENDAR

 

Pursuant to San Francisco Administrative Code Section 67.10(b) and Government Code Section 54957(b) (1), the Historic Preservation Commission will consider approval of the selected candidate recommended by the Sub-Committee of the Planning Commission and the Historic Preservation Commission.

 

SPEAKERS:  None

ACTION:        The Commission passed a ‘voice’ motion to accept the recommendation of the Commission Secretary Search Sub-Committee of Jonas Ionin as Commission Secretary.

AYES:             Antonini, Borden, Hillis, Moore, Sugaya, Wu and Fong

 

ADJOURNMENT    12:21 PM

 

ADOPTED – November 7, 2013
